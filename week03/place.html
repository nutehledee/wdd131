Nigeria

Summary

Nigeria, officially the Federal Republic of Nigeria, is a country in West Africa. It is the most populous country in Africa and the sixth-most populous country in the world. Lagos is the largest city.

Bordered by Benin, Chad, Cameroon, and Niger, with coastline on the Gulf of Guinea.
